English Version

The Goal of the Game

The goal is to place each colored number in its correct spot to restore a perfectly logical grid.

The Binokub Principle

The Bases (Columns & Colors)

Every number belongs to a "Base" (a single digit from 1 to 9). The Base is calculated by summing the number's digits until only one digit remains.
Example: For the number 78, you do 7 + 8 = 15. Then, 1 + 5 = 6. The Base is 6.
A number's Base determines its final column in the grid. Its color is your clue!

The Suites (The Grid's Logic)

The grid is not random. The numbers follow a dual mathematical rule:
- Vertically: +9 when moving one cell down.
- Horizontally: +1 when moving one cell to the right.

How to Play

  • Select a number by clicking on it.
  • Click on a cell to move the number there. A move is only valid if the number is placed in its correct final position.
  • If the destination cell is occupied by another number, the two numbers will swap places if the move is correct.

Game Modes

Survival Mode

This is a test of precision.

  • The first mistake locks the starting cell üîí and costs you points.
  • The second mistake ends the game (Game Over).
Limited Moves Mode

This is a test of efficiency.

  • Solve the puzzle before your move budget runs out.
  • A mistake costs you one move and points.
  • The Perfect Swap (when two swapped numbers both land in their correct spots) is a free move and gives a large bonus!
